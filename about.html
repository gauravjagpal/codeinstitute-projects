ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is John Smith and I am a Nutritional Advisor. Since my diagnosis, I have developed a huge passion for all things health and wellness.

Since my Crohn's disease diagnosis in 2018, I have spent most of my free time researching into ways I can heal naturally.

I came across some revolutionary science that is completely different to all of the noise we currently have in the health & wellness space, and I am extremely excited to show you what worked for me. I want everyone with auto-immune disease to know that there are ways to heal without having to adhere to strict dietary & lifestyle protocols.

I hope it can change your life, because you deserve nothing but happiness and vibrant health.

MY STRUGGLES

I felt like I was held hostage by these strict FAD diets. I jumped from diet to diet and whilst I acknowledge that a healthy diet is crucial for a healthy mind and body, it isn't going to save everyone from severe inflammatory diseases.

I was determined to find a way to heal without having to be held hostage by a diet.

As well as a healthy fibre rich diet, I also wanted to enjoy sugar, fried food, pizza, burgers, and fries (in moderation of course) without the feeling of guilt or pain. And I knew this is only possible if I think outside the box.
