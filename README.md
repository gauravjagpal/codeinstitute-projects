## Design
### Colour Scheme
I decided to use neutral colours to keep the site an easy read.

#### Background Colours:
- rgb(49, 49, 49) (header and footer background)
- rgb(234, 179, 96) (background of slogan bar at top of pages)
- rgb(202, 188, 168) (text background)

#### Button Colours:
- rgb(218, 141, 0)
